Pair Programming Discussion

By: Lawrence Yu and Silas Kwong

What is pair programming?

Pair programming is where two programmers work together on a program with one person as a driver and the other a navigator

Why is pair programming important?

It is important working in pairs to have someone collaborating and checking your work.

Compare driver and navigator?

Driver writes the code while the navigator directs what the driver should be focusing on. Its a give and take relationship!
